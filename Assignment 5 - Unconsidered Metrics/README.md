# Assignment 5 - Unconsidered Metrics

## Objective

Analyze vital signs and metrics that were not included in the primary data cleaning assignments but could provide additional clinical insights.

---

## Metrics Not Analyzed

### 1. Systolic Blood Pressure (SBP)

- **Description:** Peak arterial pressure during ventricular contraction
- **Normal Range:** 90-120 mmHg
- **Clinical Importance:** Critical for identifying hypertension and hypotension

### 2. Diastolic Blood Pressure (DBP)

- **Description:** Minimum arterial pressure between beats
- **Normal Range:** 60-80 mmHg
- **Clinical Importance:** Important for vascular health assessment

### 3. Respiratory Rate

- **Description:** Number of breaths per minute
- **Normal Range:** 12-20 breaths/min
- **Clinical Importance:** Critical for respiratory distress assessment

### 4. Temperature

- **Description:** Body temperature
- **Normal Range:** 36.1-37.2°C
- **Clinical Importance:** Fever/hypothermia detection

### 5. Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)

- **Description:** Blood oxygen levels
- **Normal Range:** 95-100%
- **Clinical Importance:** Essential for respiratory function assessment

### 6. Pain Score

- **Description:** Patient-reported pain level (0-10 scale)
- **Clinical Importance:** Triage prioritization

### 7. Level of Consciousness

- **Description:** Mental status assessment (AVPU scale)
- **Clinical Importance:** Neurological assessment
